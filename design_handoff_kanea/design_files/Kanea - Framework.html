Design system

Consistent across all three apps

Status system — colour + icon + label, never colour alone

On · Healthy Green. Power flowing, balance fine, device healthy.
Low · Attention Amber. Low balance, low battery, warning before cutoff.
Off · Fault Red. Disconnected, meter offline, open fault.

The sun-gold brand colour is reserved for identity and hero moments — it is never used to convey status, so amber stays unambiguous.

Working assumptions

Flag anything you'd change — these shape the whole build
MARKET
Ghana first. GHS (₵), English + Twi, MTN MoMo & Hubtel as the mobile-money rails. Currency & language abstracted so other markets drop in.
PRICING
Prepaid metered (the spec's recommended MVP). Balance depletes with use; zero → disconnect after grace. This is the one open decision — see below.
BRAND
“Kanea” — Twi for light / lamp — as a placeholder name. Warm, trustworthy, low-literacy memorable. Swap freely.
TONE
Trust & clarity. Big numerals, status by colour + icon + label, minimal copy, large tap targets, offline/synced state always visible.
DEVICE
Mid-range Android for both mobile apps — rendered in a phone frame. Ops is desktop-first in a browser window.
SCOPE
Hero screens built out, secondary screens stubbed. v1 out-of-scope items (appliance-level monitoring, referrals, ML) deliberately left out.
OPEN
Pricing model blocks the customer-app wallet. I've built against prepaid metered — balance in units, depletes with consumption, auto-disconnect at zero with a grace period. If you'd rather a flat monthly tier or a hybrid base-fee-plus-usage model, the wallet and billing surfaces change (everything else holds). Confirm before we deepen the wallet logic.
