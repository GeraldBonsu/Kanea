The solution

Electricity as a subscription. No hardware purchase, ever.

Kanea installs solar-plus-battery at the customer's site, owns the equipment, and charges only for power consumed. A smart meter is the spine — it meters usage and can remotely enable or disable power.

Zero upfront cost

Kanea owns the panels, battery, and inverter. Customers pay nothing to get started — just subscribe and power flows.

Pay only for kWh used

Prepaid metered model. ₵35/kWh residential, ₵32/kWh SME. Top up with MTN MoMo or Hubtel in seconds.

Never cut without warning

Low-balance SMS + app alert before any disconnect. Grace period + partial keep-alive protect the customer. Hardship hold for vulnerable households.

Why Kanea wins

Three moats. All compounding.

Each advantage reinforces the others. The smart meter creates data. The data prices risk. The prepaid model eliminates collections. Offline ops reaches where others can't.

Smart-meter spine

The meter is both the billing instrument and the remote enable/disable switch. No other residential solar player in Ghana has this at scale. It is financeability and service quality in one device.

Prepaid = zero credit risk

Power follows payment. Period. No arrears, no collections debt, no write-offs. The model self-enforces. Cash flow is predictable. Investors get a utility with the credit profile of a SaaS business.

Offline-first + USSD reach

Agents onboard customers in rural Ghana with zero data connectivity. Feature-phone customers top up via *123#. Mass-market reach that smartphone-only competitors cannot replicate.

Go-to-market

Agent-led expansion, word-of-mouth flywheel.

Each installed system is a billboard. "You saved ₵62 this month vs. diesel" is the referral that sells the next customer. Agents earn on installations; field software makes every install auditable.

Agent-led sales

Commission-based agents onboard customers door-to-door. The field app makes each installation auditable — KYC, geo-tag, device serials, commissioning checklist. Quality is built into the process.

USSD · SMS reach

*123# works on any phone with no data. Balance check, top-up, fault report. Reaches the 60%+ of Ghanaians on feature phones. SMS alerts create urgency without requiring app engagement.

Word-of-mouth flywheel

"You saved ₵62 this month vs. diesel · 99.4% uptime" — the app shows this prominently. Every satisfied customer tells neighbours. The savings story sells itself.
